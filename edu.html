Education

Institutions Attended

My educational journey has been a tapestry woven from threads of diverse cultures and perspectives. Growing up, I crisscrossed between classrooms in two countries, soaking in the unique approaches and environments each had to offer. In India, I thrived on the vibrant energy of large class sizes and the emphasis on rote learning, which instilled in me a strong foundation in core subjects. Moving to the US exposed me to smaller, discussion-based classes, encouraging critical thinking and independent learning. These contrasting experiences broadened my horizons, allowing me to appreciate the value of both traditional and progressive teaching methods. This exposure to different educational styles has instilled in me a global perspective, one that values diverse viewpoints and recognizes the strengths inherent in various approaches to learning. It's a perspective that I believe enriches not only my academic pursuits but also my understanding of the world around me.
